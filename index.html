Company Introduction

ESCO INTERNATIONAL GmbH is a global marketing and distribution company, headquartered in Switzerland, specializing in high-quality silica sand products and quartzite products. Our products are sourced from a network of mines under the management of ESCO MINERAL, as the official representative that markets and manages all operational activities in each branch country, ensuring our services and products are delivered efficiently worldwide.
